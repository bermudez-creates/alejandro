"Freedom is all I want"

As dorky as it may sound of one of my role models has been a fictional character named Luffy. Luffy has inspired me to not be afraid of chasing my dreams because he is completely without fear nor doubt of what his dreams are, his most famous quote is him shouting out exactly what his dream is. That type of fearlessness is what I admire most.

In reality my idea of freedom is the ability to get up every day and be allowed to choose how I spend my time today. My dream is to gather up skills in areas I am genuinely interested in, like art or technology and recently I've been wanting to put an engine together. I want to gather up these skills so that no matter where I am my value as a person will alwasy be high, meaning a job is secured.
